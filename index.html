NUTELLA CHOCOLATE CAKE RECIPE


It's almost Christmas Time! For this special occasion, I baked a cake for you (with Nutella!). You too can bake this cake. Find out how to bake this simple cake with the very easy to follow Recipe below. Please Enjoy!

Prep Time: 20min
Cook Time: 45min
Servings: 10
Course: Dessert
Author: Orçun Bat

Ingredients:

3 Eggs
2 Cups of Sugar
1 Cup of Natural Yoghurt
1/2 Cup of Sunflower Oil
3 Cups of White Flour
1 Packet of Baking Powder & Vanilla Sugar
1/3 Sticks of Butter
1/2 Cup of Sunflower Oil
2 Tablespoons of Nutella

Instructions:

Step 1: Crack 3 Eggs in a medium-sized bowl
Step 2: Pour 2 cups of sugar into the bowl
Step 3: Mix thoroughly until the ingredients are combined (about 2min).
Step 4: Pour 1 cup of natural yoghurt and half a cup of sunflower oil into the bowl and mix again for 2min. After that, pour 3 cups of white flour and 1 pack of both baking powder & vanilla sugar into the batter. Mix thoroughly.
Step 5: Prepare your baking tray. Smear some butter on it.
Step 6: Pour a small amount of flour into the tray and spread it evenly so the cake doesn't stick to the bowl.
Step 7: Pour half of the batter into the tray.
Step 8: Prepare the other half of the batter. Put 2 tablespoons of Nutella into the mix.
Step 9: Mix until the ingredients are combined (about 2min).
Step 10: Evenly pour the other half into the tray.
Step 11: Heat the oven to 180 degrees Celsius. Put the baking tray into the oven for about 45 Minutes.
Step 12: Take the tray out of the oven.
Step 13: Carefully remove the cake from the tray
Step 15: Cut into pieces.
Step 16: Done. Enjoy!
