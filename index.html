NUTELLA CHOCOLATE CAKE RECIPE


It's almost Christmas Time! For this special occasion, I baked a cake for you (with Nutella!). You too can bake this cake. Find out how to bake this simple cake with the very easy to follow Recipe below. Please Enjoy!

Cook Time: 45min
Servings:

Ingredients:

3 Eggs
2 Cups of Sugar
1 Cup of Natural Yoghurt

3 Cups of White Flour
1 Packet of Baking Powder & Vanilla Sugar
1/3 Sticks of Butter
1/2 Cup of Sunflower Oil

Instructions:

Step 1: Crack 3 Eggs in a medium-sized bowl
Step 2: Pour 2 cups of sugar into the bowl
Step 3: Mix thoroughly until the ingredients are combined (about 2min)
Step 4: Pour 1 cup of natural yoghurt and half a cup of sunflower oil into the bowl and mix again for 2min.
Step 5: Pour 2 cups of sugar into the bowl
